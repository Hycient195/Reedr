About

Welcome to Reedr, where we believe in making the digital world more accessible for everyone, especially those with dyslexia. Our mission is to create an inclusive online space that caters to the unique needs of individuals with dyslexia, empowering them to explore the web with ease and confidence.

Accessibility is at the heart of everything we do. We understand the challenges faced by individuals with dyslexia, and we are dedicated to providing a seamless and enjoyable online experience for everyone. Our commitment extends to designing a website that goes beyond mere functionality – we prioritize accessibility to foster a sense of belonging and empowerment.

Colors play a crucial role in web design, and at Reedr, we've carefully selected a palette with the diverse needs of our users in mind. Here's why:

High Contrast:
We use high-contrast color schemes to enhance text visibility, making it easier for individuals with dyslexia to read and comprehend content effortlessly.
Color Customization:
We believe in personalization. Our website allows users to customize colors based on their preferences and comfort, ensuring a personalized and enjoyable browsing experience.
High Contrast:
Our chosen colors enhance text legibility, promoting a clear distinction between text and background. This is especially beneficial for users with dyslexia who may experience challenges in reading.
Reduced Glare:
We've minimized bright, harsh colors that may cause visual discomfort. Our soothing color scheme aims to reduce eye strain and fatigue during prolonged screen time.
